Our Services

Discover the range of services we offer to meet your real estate needs.Real estate management involves a range of services that help property owners, investors, and landlords effectively manage their properties. These services can vary depending on the type of property (residential, commercial, industrial) and the specific needs of the client.

Property Marketing

Advertising vacant properties through various channels to attract tenants or buyers.

Tenant Screening

Verifying the background, credit history, and rental history of potential tenants.

Lease Management

Drafting, reviewing, and managing lease agreements between property owners and tenants.

Rent Collection

Collecting rent payments from tenants and ensuring timely payments.

Property Maintenance

Overseeing routine maintenance and repairs to ensure the property is in good condition.

Financial Management

Handling financial aspects such as budgeting, accounting, and financial reporting.

Tenant Relations

Addressing tenant concerns, handling complaints, and maintaining positive tenant relationships.

Property Inspections

Conducting regular inspections to assess the condition of the property and identify any needed repairs or maintenance.

Legal Compliance

Ensuring that the property complies with local and national regulations and codes.

Eviction Management

Handling the eviction process when necessary, in accordance with legal requirements.
